STEM and CODE

At STEMNCODE we believe coding program is more than sitting in front of the laptop the entire class. It is a multi-sensory process. Our coding classes are divided into physical activity, unplugged projects, logical thinking and coding. Each of these sections are incorporated into our 120 minute long classes so the kids do not feel bored. We ignite the spark of creativity and exploration in the young minds. Then we sit back and see where their uniqueness takes them with gentle guidance from our expert instructors.

Our classroom is well equipped with laptops, projector, whiteboard, tables and chairs.

Our lesson plans are based on tried and tested techniques for the past 3 years. We refer to code.org, Khan Academy and several text books to build our own curriculum. Backed by several years of industry experience, volunteering in hour of code, teaksK12.org, teaching programming in summer camps and volunteering in elementary school computer classes our instructors hand craft our curriculum to instill the love of learning and research.
